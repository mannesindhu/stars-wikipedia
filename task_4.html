HERO PRABHAS

Prabhas is a popular Telugu film actor who has appeared in over 20 films. He is known as the "Rebel Star" and is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema.

Prabhas was born on October 23, 1979 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. He is the youngest of three children and is the nephew of veteran Telugu actor Krishnam Raju.

Prabhas made his acting debut in the 2002 drama Eeswar. He had his breakthrough role in the 2004 action romance Varsham. He starred in the epic action duology Baahubali: The Beginning (2015) and Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017). He made his Bollywood debut in 2014 with Action Jackson.

HERO SR.NTR

NTR was a Telugu superstar who appeared in over 300 films, including Raju Peda (1954) and Lava Kusa (1963)

He was known for his breakthrough performances and won several awards, including the Nandi Award for Best Actor in 1970 and the Filmfare Award for Best Actor in 1972.
